About PDF Chat Assistant

What is PDF Chat Assistant?

PDF Chat Assistant is an intelligent tool that allows you to have conversations with your PDF documents. Simply upload a PDF, and start asking questions about its content. Our AI-powered system will analyze the document and provide accurate answers based on the information it contains.

How It Works

PDF Chat Assistant uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning to understand both your questions and the content of your documents. When you ask a question, our system:

  1. Analyzes your question to understand what you're looking for
  2. Searches through the document for relevant information
  3. Generates a concise, accurate answer based on the document content
  4. References the specific sections of the document used to create the answer

Technology Behind PDF Chat Assistant

Our system is built using state-of-the-art technologies:

Large Language Models (LLMs)
PDF Processing
Vector Embeddings
Flask Framework
Vector Database
Semantic Search
